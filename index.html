About Me

Master in Information Security• March 2003

Radical Approach to Develop Psychological Control in Information Security: Organization’s Perspective. Information Security standards i.e. ISO-27000, have various controls listed in various control objective. I attempt to develop controls in information security from the perspective of human behaviour. The research analyses various factors which influence, motivate an individual to breach information security that was not discussed in the standard. The research also discusses various forms of attacks which an attacker applies to target the human psychology.
